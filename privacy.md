# Privacy Policy

This application allows creators to manage and upload short-form video content to TikTok.

Stored data may include:
- TikTok OAuth access tokens
- TikTok OAuth refresh tokens
- video metadata related to uploads

This data is used only for uploading approved content to TikTok accounts authorized by the user.

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Users may request deletion of stored data by contacting:
your@email.com
